CryptoJunkie

About

Hi, I'm Mike. I'm a member of the OpenBazaar core team and 1st employee @ OB1. In my spare time, I also run CryptoJunkie.

I started CryptoJunkie because I found it difficult to keep up with the latest and greatest news across many cryptocurrency projects. The industry is growing so quickly, but it seems the platforms for discovering quality content across many projects hasn't really emerged yet. Reddit is great. Twitter is great. Medium is great. Youtube is great. Coindesk is great. But there's not really one service that curates the content across all of these services into a simple experience.

CryptoJunkie is literally the easiest way I could think of to surface crypto related news to you. Simply hit the "new tab" button and Chris Dunn's latest trading video instantly starts playing. Open another tab and you may see a funny "Bitcoin to the moon" meme. Open another tab and you may see the latest Zcash blog post. And so on...

My lovely girlfriend Mariana helps populate content into CryptoJunkie every day. She does all of the the hard work hunting for amazing content, while I perfect the UX and ship code updates. Our goal is to keep the content constantly fresh, while also mixing in some classics and a bit of surprises.

If you enjoy CryptoJunkie please make sure to tweet about it below and share with friends. We very much appreciate the support!

Cheers
Mike & Mariana
